Vehicle tax refunds

DVLA will issue an automatic refund for any full remaining months of vehicle tax to the address on the V5C, and any direct debit (DD) will also be cancelled.

If you are not in the motor trade

There are separate directions for members of the public buying or selling cars. It's important you don't transfer registration of your vehicle until you are ready to. Your dealer may help you with this process.
